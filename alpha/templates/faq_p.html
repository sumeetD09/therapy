FAQ

  • Are online therapy sessions secure and private?

    Yes, our platform uses encryption to ensure that all your information and sessions are confidential and secure in compliance with HIPAA regulations..

  • Is online therapy as effective as in-person therapy?

    Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many individuals, depending on the treatment and individual preferences.

  • What if I need to cancel or reschedule my session?

    You can easily cancel or reschedule your online therapy session through your account, but we ask for at least 24 hours’ notice to avoid any cancellation fees.

  • Can I use insurance for online therapy?

    Many insurance providers cover online therapy sessions. Please check with your provider or contact our support team to verify if your sessions are eligible for coverage..

  • What types of therapy are available online?

    We offer individual therapy, couple therapy, family therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based therapy, and more. You can choose the type that best suits your needs.

  • How long does an online therapy session last?

    Typically, an online therapy session lasts between 45-60 minutes, depending on the therapist’s structure and your individual needs.
